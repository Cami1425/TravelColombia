Fairs, Celebrations and Events

Take any preconceived ideas you have about Colombia and leave them at home: this extraordinary country will take you by surprise.
The second most biodiverse nation in the world is home to vast tracts of wildlife-rich Amazon rainforest within, while white-sand beaches, vertiginous mountains
and lush highland coffee plantations crown Colombia a place of extraordinary natural contrasts.

But Colombia is much more than the sum of its natural parts. Its kinetic cities vibrate with an energy that sets it apart from most of South America
and shows that this country has shed its history of conflict.
With adventure and infectious rhythms around virtually every corner, choosing the best places to visit in Colombia can be a challenge.
With a calendar full of celebrations, carnivals, fairs, and events for all kinds of people.

Calendar 2023

Manizales Fair
From: January 1 to January 8, 2023

Description: First and largest, important and emblematic fair of the Colombian city of Manizales and of the American continent itself. Recognized for its bullfighting season and the International Coffee Reign.
Black and White Carnival
From: January 2 to January 7, 2023

Description:The largest and most important festival in south Colombia. Origined in the fusion of multiple cultures and expressions, it is celebrated in Pasto and other municipalities of Nariño.
Buena Vida Beach
From: January 6 to January 8, 2023

Description: The beaches of Cartagena de Indias welcome you to the sixth edition of the electronic music festival: BUENAVIDA BEACH. With extraordinary music, top DJs and a stage full of life.
MCM Show
From: January 19 to January 22, 2023

Description: The M.C.M SHOW is the largest custom automobile exhibition in LatinoAmerica, in its fourth version it will take place in Bogotá at the Corferias event center.
Equine Fair
From: February 9 to February 12, 2023

Description: Breeders, exhibitors, fans in general and traders from all countries that love the Colombian Criollo Paso horse to come together in the same space. It takes place in Corferias-Bogota.
Barranquilla Carnival
From: February 1 to February 8, 2023

Description: The most important folkloric and cultural festival in Colombia, brings together emblematic expressions of the memory and identity of the people of Barranquilla.
La Solar Festival
From: February 11 to February 12, 2023

Description: It is one of the most important events of the music of Medellín, with genres of urban music, electronic music and alternative rhythms. Location: Parque Norte.
Opeth Concert
February 16th, 2023 6:00pm

Description: Opeth is a Swedish progressive metal/rock band from Stockholm, formed in 1990 by lead vocalist David Isberg. The concert will take place in The Royal Center, Bogota.
Joaquín Sabina Concert
March 1, 2023

Description: Los ciudadanos recibirán a el cantautor español Joaquín Sabina en el Movistar Arena, por su gira 'Contra todo pronóstico'. Estara en diversas ciudades de América Latina y España.
International Film Festival
March 16, 2023

Description: Film festival held in Cartagena, Colombia, which focuses mainly on the promotion of Colombian television series, Latin American films and short films.
Festival Estereo Picnic
From: March 23 to March 26, 2023

Description: The Estéreo Picnic Festival is an alternative music and cultural entertainment festival that takes place every year in the city of Bogotá, Colombia.
Expo Esports Colombia
From: March 28 to March 29, 2023

Description: Es el más completo evento de juegos electrónicos, animé, cómics, mangas y entretenimiento del país, todo en un solo lugar y apuntando a ser una de las mayores de Latinoamérica.
